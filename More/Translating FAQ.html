On the Subject of Translating Manuals

Je ne parle pas le français. あなたは日本語を話せますか？ Több fordítóra van szükségünk.

KTANE is a fun game, but it’s even cooler when it’s in your own language! Follow these instructions to translate a manual of your choice into your language:

  • If you want to translate the vanilla manual (the modules that come with the game), please join Tharagon’s effort instead. This is for modded modules.
  • Check that a translation doesn’t already exist. Press the grey ▼ button next to any manual to see the list of translations we already have.
  • Open the manual you wish to translate. Important: Open the HTML version, not the PDF.
  • Press the key combination Alt+0 (that’s the number zero, not the letter Oscar).
  • Click the highlighted areas to translate them all.
  • Press Ctrl+S to save the page and then send it to Timwi#0551 on Discord.
  • Please note that the system doesn’t save your translations for you. If you leave the page, all your work is gone, so make sure you save it first.

That’s it. Let’s see your translations!
