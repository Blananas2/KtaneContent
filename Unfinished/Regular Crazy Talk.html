On the Subject of Regular Crazy Talk

What? You’re breaking up. ... I said you’re brea— what? Say again?... What?... You’re breaking up with your girlfriend??

  • The module has a large display with a phrase on it, a small 7-segment display with up/down arrow buttons, and a big round button.
  • Pressing the up/down buttons will scroll between five phrases, each with a different digit in the 7-segment display.
  • Find which of the phrases has the digit in the display equal to the “D” value in the below table.
  • With that phrase selected, hold the button when the bomb’s countdown timer contains the digit listed in the “H” column.
  • Release the button when a digit in the timer has the “R” value that didn’t have that value when you pressed the button down.
  • The phrase may have one of the embellishments shown at the top of the table. Make sure you use the correct associated columns.
