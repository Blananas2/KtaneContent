Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Neapolitan

On the Subject of Neapolitan

Come get yalls neaneanea, polipolipoli, and tantantan!

This module contains 3 ice cream cones, resting in some ice cream holders. They have varying flavors of ice cream, toppings, and cone. There is also a tub of ice cream, with buttons to toggle the 3 sections between nea, (strawberry,) poli, (vanilla,) and tan. (Chocolate.) The traits of each ice cream cone determine two values and the operator to perform to them. Round the result, take the absolute value, and mod 15. This number determines the flavor of the neapolitan ice cream's corresponding section. Use the tables below to find the two digits and the operator. The / in the ice cream and topping tables below refer to even/odd amounts of batteries for ice cream, and ports for toppings.

If the final result is 0-4: Nea
If the final result is 5-9: Poli
If the final result is 10-14: Tan
