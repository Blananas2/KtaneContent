On the Subject of X-Rays

I can see right through you.

  • A set of three symbols is revealed in the horizontal window by an x-ray scanner. The scanner only shows a thin section of each symbol at a time.
  • The scan progresses in one of three ways: from top to bottom, from bottom to top, or alternating back and forth.
  • Two of the three symbols identify a row and a column in the big table below. Find the intersection of the relevant row and column.
  • Stay there or move to an adjacent symbol as indicated by the third symbol’s location in the small table above. The center of the table corresponds to your current location. Push the button with the symbol you moved to.
