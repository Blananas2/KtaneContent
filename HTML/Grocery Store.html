On the Subject of the Grocery Store

Yay! I love it when my mommy takes me to the grocery store!

On the display there will be a grocery store item and you must choose to add it to your cart or just pay and leave with what you have and then a new item will appear on the screen. With each item added to the cart, it will add the items price, seen on the next page, to your total. Once you come across an item that would send your total over the spending limit, starting at 200$, you must pay and leave. If you pay too early or your total goes over your spending limit, you will gain a strike and start over.
