    1. If there are more than 2 batteries and a parallel port present on the bomb, disregard the chart and press the keys as they appear on the module, going left to right, top to bottom.
    2. Otherwise, if a DVI-D port and a lit indicator labeled BOB are present on the bomb, the keys must be pressed in the order they appear in the chart going right to left.
    3. If none of the above apply, the keys must be pressed in the order they appear in the chart going left to right.
