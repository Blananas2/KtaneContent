On the Subject of Hunting

Are you the hunter? Or are you the hunted?

This module must be defused in four stages by pressing valid pictogram buttons. In each stage, determine which pictograms are invalid as follows:

Table A
1 2
3 4
Table B
4 3
2 1
  • The two pictograms depicted on the upper left of the module correspond to the row and column of an entry on the grid below.
  • If the column pictogram is displayed left of the other, refer to Table A. Otherwise, refer to Table B. In that table, find the position of the current stage number.
  • Within the cell indicated by the current row/column pictograms, as well as the cells indicated in every earlier stage, find the pictograms located at the positions found in Table A or B. These are the invalid pictograms.
  • Press any valid pictogram to advance to the next stage, which changes all the pictograms on the module. Pressing an invalid pictogram will completely reset the module.
