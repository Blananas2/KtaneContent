On the Subject of Yes and No

This is not what “Yes” means?!

The module has a button labeled “Yes” and a button labeled “No”. Initially, the buttons’ meanings are what they say. However, after a button is pressed a specific number of times, the meanings of the buttons swap (but the labels don’t).

Answer the questions correctly while keeping track of the button swaps. A list of all questions with their answers can be found in the following JSON file: http://forler.ddns.net:8000/YesAndNoAllQuestions.json

Use the following tables to determine after how many presses of each button the meanings swap:
