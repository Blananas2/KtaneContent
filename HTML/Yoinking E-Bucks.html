MOMMY GIMME YOUR CREDIT CARD I WANT THE MOUTHWASH DANCE!!!!!!!!! >:(


  • On the module you will find a credit card, a fortnight cosmetic and the price of that cosmetic (in E-Bucks)
  • If your mum notices that you have stolen money from her credit card she will scream at you (causing a strike), disable your internet (hindering you from solving the module during the next two minutes). Trying to interact with your PC during that time will make your mum REEEEE at you.
  • The module will solve if you have successfully stolen enough money to buy the cosmetic so you can get the epic victory royale (YEET).

  • First identify the cosmetic or the emote using the graphic shown on the module.
  • The price of the skin is the target price.
