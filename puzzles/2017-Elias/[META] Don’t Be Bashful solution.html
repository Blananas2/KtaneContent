Don’t Be Bashful — 24.4

Solution

The title refers to ashthebash, a.k.a. Bashley, the author of several modules, indicating that the names of his modules are the feeder answers.

The left side provides spaces for the names of puzzles, which can be uniquely filled in, giving some of the letters for the colored groups. The corresponding answers must be entered on the right side in such a place that the colored groups can be anagrammed into meaningful words.

CDO:PIANOKEYS
STALEMATE:SEMAPHORE
BESTPRICES:PAINTING
CONNECTIONS:LIGHTSOUT
NOFIZZPLEASE:COLOURFLASH
PLAYTHROUGH
ONLINECHAT

This yields a clue phrase, PLAY THROUGH ONLINE CHAT, the answer to which is another mod originally by ashthebash.
